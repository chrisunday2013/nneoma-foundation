About Us

Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation Was conceived from the love and care of a mother. Today, though, unrestrain economic inequality exists, and individuals struggle to obtain basic necessities such as health, food, clothing, housing, and education, we step in to assist and serve by filling in the blanks. We are a non-profit organisation engaged in providing a better future to underprivileged homes and communities through free medical-outreaches, health education, free community based education, scholarships for bright underprivileged students, provision of borehole water to communities without access to good water source, food distribution outreaches and basic household items distribution. Through effort and donations of partners, like minds and good people all around the world, we are able to eradicate poverty, provide food, education, medical relief, sustainable development, and protect the Environment at large expecially in less privilage parts of the country.
